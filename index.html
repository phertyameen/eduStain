Vision

At EduStain, vision is simple yet powerful, which is to empower the community through quality and holistic education. We firmly believe that education, when comprehensive and all-encompassing, can break the cycle of underdevelopment, reduce crime rates, and instill values for a brighter future in the community, state, and country.

Mission

To empower the community through quality and holistic education. The organization believes that comprehensive education can break the cycle of underdevelopment, reduce crime rates, and instill values for a brighter future in the community, state, and country. Therefore, Edustain is focused on providing education that is not only of high quality but also encompasses various aspects to contribute to the overall development of individuals and the community.

What We Do

01

Business Management Support

The YBI Foundation extends its expertise to businesses in their initial stages by offering business management services. This includes assistance in developing business plans, providing strategic direction, and offering counseling to help these businesses establish a strong foundation for success

02

Championing Innovative Solutions

YBIF actively engages in identifying and promoting innovative solutions to address global challenges outlined in the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) such as implementation of creative and effective approaches to poverty eradication, quality education, gender equality, and environmental sustainability.

03

Fostering Partnerships

YBI foundation works towards building strong and meaningful partnerships with various stakeholders, including governmental bodies, non-profit organizations, businesses, and local communities. Collaborative efforts are essential in maximizing impact and creating a united front to tackle complex issues related to well-being, education, and social equality.

04

Empowering Communities

YBI Foundation is dedicated to empowering communities by providing them with the necessary tools, resources, and educational opportunities. This could involve initiatives aimed at skill development, capacity building, and creating a supportive environment that enables individuals to take charge of their own development and contribute to the well-being of their communities.

05

Supporting Environmental Initiatives

Recognizing the importance of environmental sustainability, the foundation actively supports initiatives and projects that address environmental challenges. This may include activities related to conservation, renewable energy, climate change mitigation, and promoting environmentally friendly practices to contribute to a healthier and more sustainable planet.

06

Promoting Social Equality

YBI Foundation is committed to promoting social equality by advocating for and supporting initiatives that address issues related to gender equality, inclusivity, and social justice. This involves projects that focus on creating equal opportunities for all individuals, regardless of gender, race, or background, fostering a society where everyone can thrive and contribute to positive change.

Fueling Innovation: Sokoto Startup Boost

Imagine a world where every entrepreneur thrives,
Communities flourish,
And the promise of a brighter future is fulfilled.

This is the story of YBI Foundation — a narrative of empowerment, innovation, and compassion.

In 2023, YBI Foundation proudly announced a substantial financial commitment, providing one million grants specifically tailored for startup ventures led by entrepreneurs in Sokoto. This initiative aims to fuel innovation, economic growth, and job creation within the region.

Empowering Entrepreneurs Through Education

YBI Foundation has organized and funded educational training programs that focus on enhancing the capabilities of entrepreneurs. These workshops aim to equip participants with knowledge and skills crucial for effective leadership, efficient management, and financial literacy.

Education Empowerment Initiatives

YBI Foundation champions a noble cause by sponsoring 60 Educational Support Initiatives. This initiative focuses on providing educational opportunities to orphans, street children, and vulnerable children. By offering support in the form of scholarships, access to educational resources, and mentorship. The foundation aims to positively impact the lives and futures of these underserved individuals.

Northern Entrepreneurial Flourish

YBI Foundation has extended its support to Northern Entrepreneurs by offering provisional printing services. This includes the production of label stickers and Nylon bags, contributing to the branding and packaging needs of these entrepreneurs. Additionally, financial grants have been allocated to facilitate their entrepreneurial endeavors.

The Executive Vice Chairman

A result-oriented community development practitioner, climate change advocate, and a farmer, Suleiman was the first State Coordinator for Nigeria Youth for Peace Initiative, a youth-led organization working on conflict prevention and peacebuilding processes across communities in Nigeria and also works towards localization of the United Nations Security Council Resolution 2250 on Youth peace and security. He is a member of the Nigerian Society of Engineers as well as the Institution of Engineering and Technology.
